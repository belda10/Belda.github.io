IRONMAN

Iron Man, created by Stan Lee, Larry Lieber, Don Heck, and Jack Kirby, first appeared in Tales of Suspense #39 in 1963. Tony Stark, a wealthy industrialist and genius inventor, builds a high-tech suit of armor to escape captivity and becomes the superhero known as Iron Man.

With his sharp wit and extraordinary technology, Iron Man has become a beloved character in the Marvel Universe, leading the Avengers and appearing in numerous films. His journey from a self-centered playboy to a hero embodies themes of redemption and responsibility.
