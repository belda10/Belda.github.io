DR. STRANGE

Dr. Stephen Strange, formerly a neurosurgeon, becomes the Sorcerer Supreme after a tragic car accident severely damages his hands. Desperate to heal, he seeks out alternative healing methods, leading him to the Ancient One.

Through rigorous training, he learns the mystic arts, unlocking powers that allow him to protect the Earth from mystical threats. Dr. Strange is known for his mastery of magic, the use of artifacts like the Eye of Agamotto, and his role as a member of the Avengers.

His character debuted in "Strange Tales" #110 in July 1963, created by writer Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko. Since then, Dr. Strange has become a pivotal figure in the Marvel Universe, featuring in numerous comic series, animated adaptations, and blockbuster films.
