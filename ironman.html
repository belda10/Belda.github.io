IRON MAN

Iron Man, also known as Tony Stark, is a fictional superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. He was created by writer Stan Lee, developed by scripter Larry Lieber, and designed by artists Don Heck and Jack Kirby.x

Tony Stark is a wealthy industrialist and genius inventor who creates a high-tech suit of armor to save his life and later uses it to protect the world as Iron Man. His character explores themes of responsibility, redemption, and the consequences of one's actions, particularly through his transformation from a self-centered weapons manufacturer to a hero who prioritizes the greater good.

The character has become one of the central figures in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), portrayed by Robert Downey Jr. in several films, starting with "Iron Man" (2008). Stark's journey through the MCU showcases his development, struggles with personal demons, and his pivotal role in the fight against threats like Thanos in "Avengers: Infinity War" and "Avengers: Endgame."
