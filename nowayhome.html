SPIDER-MAN NO WAY HOME

Spider-Man, created by writer Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko, first appeared in "Amazing Fantasy" #15 in 1962. Since then, he has become one of the most beloved superheroes in the Marvel universe.

Origin Story

Peter Parker, a high school student, gains spider-like abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider. He learns that with great power comes great responsibility, a mantra that guides him throughout his adventures.

Notable Villains

    Green Goblin

    Doctor Octopus

    Venom

    Sandman

Media Adaptations

Spider-Man has been featured in numerous films, animated series, and video games, making him a pop culture icon. Notable films include Sam Raimi's "Spider-Man" trilogy and the recent MCU adaptations.
