THE AVENGERS

About the Avengers

The Avengers are a team of superheroes in the Marvel Comics universe, first assembled in "The Avengers" #1 in 1963. Created by writer Stan Lee and artist Jack Kirby, the team has become a cornerstone of Marvel's storytelling.

Notable Members

  • Iron Man (Tony Stark): The genius billionaire with a heart of gold.
  • Captain America (Steve Rogers): The super-soldier fighting for justice.
  • Thor: The God of Thunder wielding Mjolnir.
  • Black Widow (Natasha Romanoff): A master spy and combatant.
  • Hulk (Bruce Banner): The green giant with immense strength.

Iconic Moments

The Avengers have had many unforgettable moments in both comics and films, including:

  • Their first battle against Loki and the Chitauri in "The Avengers" (2012).
  • The epic showdown against Thanos in "Avengers: Endgame" (2019).
