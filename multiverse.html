DR. STRANGE IN THE MULTIVERSE OF MADNESS

Join Dr. Stephen Strange as he navigates through the multiverse, facing new threats and discovering dark secrets. This thrilling sequel takes you deeper into the mystical arts and explores the consequences of altering reality.

Directed by Sam Raimi, the film features Benedict Cumberbatch reprising his role as Dr. Strange, alongside Elizabeth Olsen as Wanda Maximoff and newcomers like Xochitl Gomez as America Chavez.

Get ready for mind-bending visuals and an exciting storyline that challenges the boundaries of the Marvel Cinematic Universe!
